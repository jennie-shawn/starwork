### General Workflow

1. Put source materials, reference links, and user-provided materials in `{{paths.references}}`; treat them as read-only by default.
2. Put AI-generated but unapproved work in `{{paths.drafts}}`.
3. Put user-approved outputs in `{{paths.final}}`, or in another formal source declared by the project.
4. Do not silently treat drafts as final outputs.
