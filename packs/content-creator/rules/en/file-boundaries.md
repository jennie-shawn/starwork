### Content Creator File Boundaries

- `{{paths.account_profile}}`: account positioning, target audience, content thesis, and expression boundaries.
- `{{paths.ideas}}`: candidate ideas, inspiration, title directions, and opportunities to validate.
- `{{paths.materials}}`: raw materials, cases, links, screenshot notes, and audience feedback quotes.
- `{{paths.drafts}}`: unpublished posts, scripts, storyboards, title options, and cover directions.
- `{{paths.published}}`: published or approved publishing records; this is the default formal source for this Pack.
- `{{paths.review}}`: post-publishing data, judgment, lessons, and next content recommendations.

Do not treat materials as drafts, and do not treat drafts as publishing records.
