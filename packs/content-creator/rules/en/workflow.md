### Content Production Flow

1. Read `{{paths.account_profile}}` first to confirm positioning, target audience, and expression preferences.
2. Put new ideas in `{{paths.ideas}}`; do not jump directly into final drafts.
3. Put materials, cases, screenshots, links, and audience wording in `{{paths.materials}}`.
4. Put drafts, scripts, and single-piece production work in `{{paths.drafts}}`.
5. After publishing, update `{{paths.published}}`.
6. Once data is available, review it in `{{paths.review}}` and feed the next idea suggestions back into `{{paths.ideas}}`.
